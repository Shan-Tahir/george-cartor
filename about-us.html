Welcome to PLC Trucking, where we proudly uphold a legacy of dedication and pride, inspired by my father, Paul L. Carter, owner of Waukegan Cartage in Naperville. Each mile we drive honors the enduring spirit of Paul L. Carter. Thank you for choosing us, where tradition meets reliability on the road.
